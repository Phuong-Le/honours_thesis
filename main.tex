\part{Results: Whole disease analysis}{
    The next two chapters report the results from whole disease analysis of genomic location effect (\gls{gle}) and sequence context effect (\gls{sce}), respectively. In particular, this part dwells on \textbf{whether} GLE and SCE are informative about different cancer types, and explores \textbf{in what way} they are informative. This acts as a basis for training the \gls{classifier} in chapter \ref{ml}.}
